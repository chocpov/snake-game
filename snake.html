Snake Game

1. Objective

The goal is to control the snake, eat the red food blocks, and grow as long as possible without hitting the walls or itself.

2. Controls

Use the following keyboard arrows to control the snake's direction:

  • ⬅️ Left Arrow: Move Left
  • ⬆️ Up Arrow: Move Up
  • ➡️ Right Arrow: Move Right
  • ⬇️ Down Arrow: Move Down

3. Scoring System

Each red block the snake eats adds 1 point to your score. The score is displayed at the bottom of the canvas.

4. Game Over Conditions

The game ends if:

  • The snake runs into the wall.
  • The snake runs into its own body.

An alert box will display your final score upon game over.

5. Tips & Strategies

  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid trapping the snake.
  • Try to stay away from the edges unless necessary.
  • Control speed by modifying the setInterval timing in the JavaScript code.
